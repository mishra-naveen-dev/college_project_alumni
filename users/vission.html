Vision Statement

To foster a lifelong relationship between the alumni and the college, and to create a community that supports the personal and professional growth of its members.

To establish a lifelong relationship between the alumni and the college, and to create a network of engaged and supportive alumni who positively impact their communities.

Mission Statement

The Alumni Association Society of Sushila Devi Bansal College exists to connect, engage, and empower alumni by providing opportunities for networking, professional development, and social engagement.

Our mission is to build a strong alumni community that enriches the lives of its members and enhances the reputation of the college.

The Alumni Association Society of Sushila Devi Bansal College is committed to connecting, engaging, and empowering alumni by fostering a sense of community, providing opportunities for personal and professional development, and encouraging philanthropy.

Our mission is to enrich the lives of our alumni, to promote the value of a Sushila Devi Bansal College education, and to serve as advocates for the college

Objectives

To connect alumni with each other and the college through regular events, reunions, and online networking opportunities

To support the personal and professional growth of our members through professional development workshops, mentoring programs, and career resources.

To give back to the college by providing financial support and volunteering our time and expertise to help current students and the college community.

To promote the achievements and successes of our members, and to enhance the reputation of the college through alumni-led initiatives and programs.

To build a culture of engagement and belonging among our members, and to create a welcoming and inclusive community for all alumni.

To cultivate a vibrant alumni community that connects alumni with each other and the college through networking events, social media, and other engagement opportunities.

To provide resources and opportunities for alumni to continue their personal and professional growth, including mentorship, continuing education, and career support.

To encourage philanthropy and volunteerism among alumni by promoting giving to the college, and by offering opportunities for alumni to give back to the college and to their communities.

To promote the value and prestige of a [college name] education by highlighting the achievements of our alumni, and by showcasing the impact of the college on the lives of our alumni and their communities.

To serve as advocates for the college by building relationships with prospective students, families, and donors, and by communicating the college's mission, values, and achievements to a wider audience.
